Pizza Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).
  2. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  3. Add cheese and your favorite toppings.
  4. Bake for 10–15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden.
  5. Slice and enjoy!

Noodles Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. Drain the noodles and return them to the pot.
  3. Add the butter to the hot noodles and mix until the butter melts.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Optional: Add Parmesan cheese for extra flavor.
  6. Serve and enjoy your butter noodles!
